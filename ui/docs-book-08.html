Notebook depth: The AUDIT Economy

Value — AUDIT

The mechanism promised self-funding rewards. The unit is AUDIT.

PropertyMeaning
Supply disciplineNew supply only through mechanism output — chiefly confirmed success-path (positive path) work
Minter sealedOne-way lock after bootstrap; no admin inflation for disputes
GenesisFirst tokens earned on first real confirm — zero before that

The token does not encode audit logic. It encodes trust in supply: if it moved, the mechanism authorized it.

Escrow — money already there

BucketMeaning
BountyAUDIT locked at submit — before assign or verdict
ReservesSlices for discoverer pay on reproduced miss — never fresh mint

On a pass, auditor payment waits out the challenge window — lasting stake. Escrow turns you will be paid if the check holds into a fact on chain.

Why they are one chapter

Value without escrow is a coin with nothing to lock. Escrow without a disciplined token is a vault someone can inflate.

Together they answer what chapter 4 named: skin in the game with money that already exists under published rules.

Before you turn the page

AUDIT — mechanism-minted; supply grows on authorized output only.

Bounty — locked at submit; magnitude public from day one.

Reserves — discoverer payout source; never mint to make whole.
