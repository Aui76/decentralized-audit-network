The auditor's reward

When does the auditor receive AUDIT — and why not** at verdict?

Story context

  • Book ch 8 · Value & escrow

Paid at confirm, not verdict

After PASS, challenge window opens — strangers may file proof-bearing claims. Payment waits until window elapses clean (lasting stake).

On `confirmAudit`:

  1. Auditor receives nextPositiveBlockReward() — activity-led mint (Activity-led reward).
  2. Treasury share on same confirm fills escrow + LP (Where mint splits · Escrow).
MomentEconomic meaning
VerdictPass/fail committed — probe period may still run
ConfirmEconomic chapter closes — mint or pre-mint void path ends

Paying at verdict would remove the public probe that makes PASS credible to strangers.

After confirm

If claim reproduces later → discoverer paid from reserves (The discoverer's reward).

Auditor's positive-block mint is not clawed back.
