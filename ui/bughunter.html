Quickstart

Bug-hunter

Find a bug the auditor missed during the challenge period, prove it, and claim its bounty.

1

Find a bug run the tool first

Browse the audits and pick one still in its challenge period (use "Open to challenge" to filter). Run a tool that fails on the contract — that produces the result root your claim needs.

Browse audits →
2

File a claim

Prove the bug with the tool and file. Filing escrows a stake and pauses the audit. You must be registered, and not the contract's protocol or its original auditor.

Open manage →
3

Wait for the re-check

The protocol opens a dispute; an independent auditor re-checks the original contract — re-running your tool on the same deployed code.

4

Get paid

If the bug reproduces, you're paid from reserves — not new supply. Track the audit's status as it resolves.

Your stake in the outcome

An unprovable claim costs you

If the bug can't be reproduced on the original contract — or the claim is never taken up and its window runs out — you lose your stake. File only what you can prove.
