Bounty bands

Where does this audit's locked bounty rank** among peers — without changing payout?

Story context

  • Book ch 14 · Bounties & pricing

Bounty bands rank one audit's locked bounty against other economically active bounties. Thermodynamic signal for cards and hints — not on-chain payout or treasury routing (Settlement vs signal). Positive-block treasury split lives in CellEscrow and IssuanceModule (Where mint splits · Escrow architecture).

Cohort percentiles

From sorted active bounties, compute { p25, p50, p75, activeCount }. Classify each audit:

BandRuleMeaning
top_quartilemagnitude >= p75Hot cohort — may trigger integrity suggest
midmagnitude >= p25 and < p75Middle of active bounties
lowmagnitude < p25, or activeCount === 0Below p25 or empty cohort

Active bounties — bounty locked at submit for rows in economically active states (e.g. in window, in block). Empty cohort → all bands low.

Integrator use

Rebuild activeBounties from chain: bounty field on each active row. Precompute { p25, p50, p75 } once per tick.

On InBlock cards, pass bounty + bountyDistribution (or accept a missing-data warning). Bands gate integrity-review suggestions and flow copy — not mint or discoverer paid.

Organ: cell/indexer/bountyDistribution.mjs · test: npm run test:organs-o8.

What bands do not do

  • Do not change LP / escrow / founder split on confirm
  • Do not alter nextPositiveBlockReward()
  • Do not replace discoverer boost (The discoverer's reward)
