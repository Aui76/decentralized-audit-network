Maturity

How far along** is an audit or participant — without that label becoming a payout gate?

Story context

  • Book ch 16 · Reputation signals
  • Book ch 22 · Reading the chain

How far a subject has moved through its lifecycle. Derived off-chain by derivePhaseAnimation from cell events — advisory, never a gate.

Bands and phases

An audit and each participant carry a maturity band — coarse position from new to established. Detail lives in phases:

FieldMeaning
phasesLifecycle phases the subject currently occupies
phaseHistory[]Ordered record of phases passed through
phaseVelocityHow fast the subject moves between phases
sequenceHintsExpected next phases, given history

Participant phases (protocol, auditor, tool) use the same pattern — deriveProtocolPhase, deriveToolPhase. Full role tables: Participant phases (O3).

What it is for

Maturity makes "how far along" legible — a new auditor vs an established one, submission vs deep in the challenge window. It feeds the Bob card (maturityBand) and pairs with Network flow for the network's sense of motion.

It changes no verdict and no payout.
