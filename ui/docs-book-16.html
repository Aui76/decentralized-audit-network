Integrators render on cards. The cell never reads them back.

Suggest, never settle

Only objective on-chain facts gate pay, slash, or verdict. A derived signal may suggest — a buggy indexer cannot mis-settle.

Health synthesizes the network picture — advisory only; never confirms, mints, or slashes.
