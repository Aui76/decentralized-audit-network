Escrow equilibrium

Why 24.9% inflow vs ~16–17% outflow — and what breaks if reserves are wrong?

Story context

  • Book ch 8 · Value & escrow

Escrow is a margin buffer — not the primary APY engine.

Design target

FlowRough target
OutflowLong-run ~16–17% of normal mint per audit (calibrated mix)
Inflow24.9% of treasury deposit per confirm

Inflow slightly above outflow → reserves build slowly.

When LP cap binds, more mint queues in timelocked escrow → slower immediate LP depth, stronger reserve for floor / discoverer / integrity.

Failure modes

ProblemSymptom
Too high escrow captureGames draining or starving queues
Too low reserveNo equilibrating power in depression or hunt bursts

Recalibrate at mainnet from telemetry — Mainnet calibration.

Principle

Market price of AUDIT is outside contracts. Chain fixes when mint/draws allowed and which bucket. Escrow smooths; issuance rewards survived work.
