Issuance network states

When the network is depressed — what extra AUDIT (floor supplement, green-light) can confirm trigger on top of Activity-led reward?

Story context

  • Book ch 10 · Issuance & positive blocks

When activity sags or escrow cannot cover the floor, issuance adds pay beyond the base positive-block reward — escrow supplements and, in extremis, conditional fresh mint.

Design: Adaptive issuance · base mint formula: Activity-led reward.

Depression floor

When Depression → payFloorSupplement from general escrow (not fresh mint).

MechanismMeaning
payFloorSupplementExtra AUDIT on confirm — capped by maxEscrowDrawdownPerAudit
floorDecayBps + failedRecoveryAttemptsFloor strength decays — no permanent bailout

Green-light backstop

When escrow cannot cover full floor and reserves are depleted → `greenLightMintAllowed()` may allow fresh mint to the completing auditor.

Treat green-light as conditional backstop, not ordinary issuance.
