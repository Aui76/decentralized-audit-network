Quickstart

Protocol

Post a contract, get it audited, settle on-chain.

1

Prepare

A deployed contract, a spec, a registered tool, and AUDIT for the bounty.

2

Approve

Allow the network to hold your bounty in escrow.

3

Submit

Post the contract and bounty. The audit goes live.

Open the submit tool →
4

Accept your auditor

An auditor is assigned. Accept to start the review. Stay silent and it auto-accepts after the window.

Open manage →
5

Wait for the challenge period

The auditor reviews your contract and gives a verdict. Then a fixed waiting period opens — time for anyone to find a missed bug before the auditor is paid.

6

Confirm

If the waiting period ends with no bug found, confirm to pay the auditor and close the audit.

Open manage →

Branch — a bug was found on the contract

Ask for an independent re-check (dispute)

Someone says your contract has a bug the auditor missed. Have a different, independent auditor re-check the original contract — their result decides whether the bug is real.
