Auditor pay — after the window

Not paid at verdict. On pass → wait challenge window → confirm → mint on success path (positive path).

Discoverer pay — boost, cap, floor

Target boosts with relevant reputation record. Cap ties to escrow depth. Floor ties to fraction of locked bounty.

Extra above bounty comes from reserves first. Auditor’s positive block is not clawed back.

FAIL at audit (Gate B) → 1× boost path (no PASS to miss).

Claim stake and the deadline

Stake scales with bounty — spam on rich lines hurts.

Past claim resolution window → expiry pays discoverer zero; stake slashed. Liveness, not automatic guilt on original auditor.

Before you turn the page

Bounty — locked at submit; public magnitude.

Auditor reward — mint at confirm, not verdict.

Discoverer reward — reserves; boost × bounty; cap and floor.

Claim stake — scales with bounty.

Deadline — expired claim pays zero.
