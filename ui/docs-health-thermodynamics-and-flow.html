Network flow

How "hot" is the network* — and where is flow allowed to suggest* (never settle)?

Story context

  • Book ch 22 · Reading the chain

The network's activity read as energy — how much value is moving, and how fast. Derived off-chain from the bounty and audit stream; advisory, never a gate.

Not here: full signal catalog or signal-to-action rule — Signals. Issuance EMA and mint size — Activity-led reward.

Flow

The central reading is `flow` — an audit's or the network's energy:

FieldMeaning
magnitudeValue in motion (bounties locked, audits in flight)
bountyBandPercentile band among economically active bounties (Bounty bands)

Large bounties through many audits → hot. Little locked value → idle. Flow is the thermodynamic axis; Maturity is "how far along."

Phase A — the one place flow is consulted

When magnitude and bountyBand are high (top_quartile), the Bob card may raise `integrityReviewSuggested` — a prompt that a large, hot audit may merit an integrity review.

It is a suggestion: the opener still calls openIntegrityReview on-chain (Integrity review). Flow never opens a review itself.

StepLayerMeaning
1Off-chain flowCard raises integrityReviewSuggested
2Human / protocol UIDecides whether to open review
3On-chainopenIntegrityReview — fact, not signal

What flow is not

Flow is…Flow is not…
A card hint for integrity reviewPayout or mint input
Derived from bounty + audit streamIssuance EMA (Activity-led reward)
Advisory thermodynamicsA settlement gate — see Signals

Issuance follows the activity curve (The positive block), independent of how hot the network looks.
