Contract surface

What can I call and read on AuditCell** — views, entrypoints, modules, events — without decoding behaviour from Solidity?

The cell's external surface — what integrators read, what actors call, and what organs subscribe to. Behaviour (states, gates, overlays) lives in the Flows book; money in the Economy book; Bob cards in Integration. This leaf is the index of the surface itself.
